FAQ

A selection of frequently asked questions

    What guarantee do you offer?

    We offer a 3 year guarantee against our workmanship.
    We do not guarantee against warping, splitting, cupping, cracking or faults caused by poor maintenance. Our gates will last a long time if correctly looked after and most faults occur due to poor maintenance.

    How much is delivery?

    We offer FREE delivery on all of our products to Mainland UK (excluding Scottish Highlands).
    For anywhere else please contact us for a price.

    Who deliver's the gates?

    We deliver ALL gates ourselves. This ensures that the gates get to you promptly and safely.

    What timber is used in the gates?

    We use Scandanavian Redwood in all of our gates. This is responsibly sourced and carefully selected to ennsure the best quality for you.

    Can I install these myself?

    Yes absolutley! Gates are very simple to install as long as everything is planned our correctly and you take your time. If you need and advice just give us a call of tweet us @MidlandGates

    How long will it take to receive my gates?

    Manufacture and delivery is just 3-4 weeks from ordering.
